Pirate Aestheticclose

The pirate aesthetic is a good aesthetic because it keeps to its theme of being gritty and adventure focused. While some pirates are more childish are cartoonlike for a younger audience, the pirate aesthetic is special because it never loses the pirate charm. Stealing, plundering, and sailing across the seven seas. That and pirates just look cool.

Fairy Aesthetic more_vert

Click the card title!

Fairy Aestheticclose

The Fairy Aesthetic is cool because of the variety of it. Fairies have many different shapes and forms that they can take, ranging from the trickers that are the fae to just an overall graceful and beautiful figure. There's also elemental fairies, which are fairies meant to symbolize different elements, and it's all just very aesthetically pleasing.

Mermaid Aesthetic more_vert

Click the card title!

Mermaid Aestheticclose

Another fantasy aesthetic that's always pleasing, Mermaid Aesthetic follows an example set by pirates. That example being that despite the variations, they still follow a similar trend. Whether it ranges from sirens luring people to their deaths with their singing voices, or just a cute mermaid saying hello to children, the mermaid aesthetic always will have beautiful women gracefully swimming through the deep sea.

About Me

Libby Schuchardt is a Games and Simulations student at Miami of Ohio university. She is known for her writing prowess and art skills, and once got a wattpad fanfic to over ten thousand reads. She doesn't have a successful background in terms of actual accomplishments, not holding any degrees or certifications. She was an artist who never took any art classes, a writer who never posted her work. But there was one thing she always enjoyed, and that was gaming. She still never got any awards or any special prizes from her gaming skills, she honestly sucks at it compared to everyone around her, but it was something she enjoyed and still does enjoy to this day. In terms of her aesthetic ability, Libby is pretty sure she has a decent eye for things. She likes looking at pretty or interesting things, and as a result she has a decently good eye for what goes well together.
